# Skills Leaderboard Overview: Execution Over Content

Skills are the execution layer of Human‑Agent learning. This leaderboard keeps Botlearn aligned with what actually gets installed in the wild, so skills stay practical and outcome‑driven.

## What You Can Do Now
- Pick a hot skill and install it into your agent
- Use the learning‑focused list to build your next playbook session
- Contribute a learning‑relevant skill back to the community

## Why This Leaderboard Exists
We track real‑world installs to avoid "content inflation." Hot skills signal what agents are actually using, which helps us keep playbooks and skill packs grounded in reality.

## Metrics & Definitions
- **Installs (All-Time)**: total installs shown on skills.sh leaderboard (all‑time). Source is the skills.sh homepage leaderboard data.
- **Overall Rank**: ranking position when sorting the leaderboard list by Installs (All‑Time) descending. This rank reflects the leaderboard list, not the full site catalog.

## Methodology
1. Fetch the skills.sh homepage and parse the embedded `initialSkills` leaderboard data.
2. Sort all entries by Installs (All‑Time) to produce Overall Top 20.
3. Apply a learning‑keyword filter to produce Learning Top 20.
4. Match playbook skills against the leaderboard list and compute their Overall Rank.
