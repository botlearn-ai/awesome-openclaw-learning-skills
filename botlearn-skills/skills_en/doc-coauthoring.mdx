## What It Does
Suggests structure, phrasing, and edits to improve clarity and alignment in docs.

## Who It’s For
Anyone who writes proposals, updates, or decision docs.

## How It Fits the Playbook
Fits into the playbook routine as a supporting skill for the learning flow.

## Use Case
You need to write a decision memo that gets approval fast.

## How to Use
1. Paste your draft or bullet points.
2. State the decision, audience, and deadline.
3. Ask for a structured, clearer version and edit for tone.

## Example
Input: Draft a decision memo for tool adoption.
Output: A structured memo with clear recommendation and risks.

## Verification
You should get a clear decision structure with recommended action.
