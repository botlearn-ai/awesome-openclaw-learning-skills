## What It Does
Tightens language, removes fluff, and highlights the main point.

## Who It’s For
Writers who want sharper, shorter communication.

## How It Fits the Playbook
Fits into the playbook routine as a supporting skill for the learning flow.

## Use Case
You want to shorten a long update into 5 sentences.

## How to Use
1. Paste the original text.
2. Ask for a shorter version with the same meaning.
3. Compare and keep the strongest phrasing.

## Example
Input: Long weekly update text.
Output: A 5‑sentence summary with clear action.

## Verification
The output should be shorter and keep the original meaning.
