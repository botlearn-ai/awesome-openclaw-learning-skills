## 功能说明
Turns raw notes into structured summaries you can revisit later.

## 适用人群
Learners who want to capture and recall key discussion points.

## 与 Playbook 的结合方式
Use it in the “Capture” step to record insights and in “Connect” to link notes.

## 使用场景
You had a learning chat and need to summarize takeaways.

## 使用步骤
1. Paste raw notes or a transcript snippet.
2. Ask for a concise summary and key takeaways.
3. Save the summary and link it to related notes.

## 示例
Input: Notes from a study group call.
Output: Summary + 3 key takeaways + 1 next action.

## 验证方式
You should have a concise summary that is easy to reuse later.
