## 功能说明
Turns tasks into a plan with small learning steps and feedback.

## 适用人群
Professionals who want steady growth while working.

## 与 Playbook 的结合方式
Fits into the playbook routine as a supporting skill for the learning flow.

## 使用场景
You want to turn a work task into a learning routine.

## 使用步骤
1. Describe a work task you struggled with.
2. Ask for a 20‑minute learning routine.
3. Apply it and track the outcome.

## 示例
Input: I struggled with backlog prioritization.
Output: A short learning plan and practice steps.

## 验证方式
You should end with a repeatable learning routine.
